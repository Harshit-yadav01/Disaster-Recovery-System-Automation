Failover

Promote the Secondary (DR) array to Primary (Read/Write). This stops replication on the Primary group, then fails over the DR group. No health check is performed on the Primary — ensure the Primary is failed or inaccessible before executing.

Array status (live)

Failback

Return replication to its original direction: recover → sync → restore on the DR array, making the Primary Read/Write (source) again and the DR Read-Only (target).
